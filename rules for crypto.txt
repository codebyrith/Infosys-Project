# 📘 CRYPTO INVESTMENT MANAGER – SIMPLE NOTES

---

# 1️⃣ Daily Return

**What it means:**

How much the price changed in one day.

**Formula:**

```
(today price - yesterday price) / yesterday price
```

If positive → price increased
If negative → price decreased

---

# 2️⃣ Average Daily Return

**What it means:**

Average growth per day over many days.

It tells:

👉 On average, how much this crypto grows daily.

---

# 3️⃣ Total Return

**What it means:**

Overall growth from first day to last day.

```
(last price - first price) / first price
```

This shows total profit percentage.

---

# 4️⃣ Risk (Standard Deviation)

**What it means:**

How much price moves up and down.

Big movement → High risk
Small movement → Low risk

---

# 5️⃣ Base ETA (Future Prediction)

**What it means:**

If past trend continues, how much it may grow in next 30 days.

```
average daily return × 30 days
```

Simple future guess.

---

# 6️⃣ Recent Trend

**What it means:**

Average growth of last 7 days.

Shows short-term movement.

---

# 7️⃣ Adjusted ETA

**What it means:**

Future prediction using:

* Long-term average
* Recent short-term trend

It mixes both to give better prediction.

If positive → price may increase
If negative → price may decrease

---

# 8️⃣ Weighted Return (Portfolio)

When we invest in 3 cryptos:

Example:
40% BTC
30% ETH
30% BNB

Weighted return means:

Total expected return of full investment.

It combines all three returns.

---

# 9️⃣ Weighted ETA

Total expected future growth of full portfolio.

---

# 🔟 Weighted Risk

Total risk of full portfolio.

If portfolio has risky coins → risk increases.

---

# 1️⃣1️⃣ Score (How Best Mix Is Chosen)

Your formula:

```
Score = Weighted ETA − (Weighted Risk × 0.5)
```

Meaning:

More future growth → good
More risk → bad

System chooses mix with highest score.

---

# 1️⃣2️⃣ Risk Levels

Based on price movement:

High movement → HIGH risk
Medium movement → MEDIUM risk
Low movement → LOW risk

---

# 1️⃣3️⃣ Trend

If Adjusted ETA > 0 → UPWARD trend
If Adjusted ETA < 0 → DOWNWARD trend

---

# 1️⃣4️⃣ Investment Mix

Mix means:

How you divide money between cryptos.

Example:

40% BTC
0% BTC
60% ETH

System tests many combinations and picks best one.

---

# 1️⃣5️⃣ Email Alert

System sends email if:

* Risk becomes HIGH
  OR
* Trend becomes DOWNWARD
